# Getting Started

Welcome to the TN HMIS Wiki (Unofficial)!

## About TN HMIS
A web-based health management information system built by [Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC)](https://www.cdac.in/) under [National Health Mission (NHM)](https://nhm.gov.in/) for hospital management including Registration, OPD, IPD, Laboratory, Pharmacy, etc.

The services are hosted in 3 web domains for services provided by 3 directorates under the main domain `tn.gov.in`.

- https://tnhmis.tn.gov.in/ provide services for Directorate of Public Health and Preventive Medicine (DPH)
- https://tnhmis.dms.tn.gov.in/ provide services for Directorate of Medical and Rural Services (DMS)
- https://tnhmis.dmer.tn.gov.in/ provide services for Directorate of Medical Education and Research (DMER)

## Login Page
![Login Page](/src/assets/images/login-page.png)

Each users are assigned a unique username which could be their name or their phone number. Based on their designation, module accessibility will vary.

For example: doctors can only access Doctor's Desk (OPD/IPD/EMR/MIS, etc)

## Home Page
![Home Page](/src/assets/images/home-page.png)

Home page is structured as following:
- Main menu which lists all the **available modules** for the user.
- Tab view presents each service as a tab. All the services are presented within a single main page. Clicking on a service will open it up in a tab under the main page.
